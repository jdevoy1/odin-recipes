Baked Chicken Thighs

Description

Simple baked chicken thigh recipe using cooking spray and seasoning blend. It is designed to be very easy, for those days where food needs to get made in as few steps as humanly possible. Just spray the chicken, or use oil if you prefer, pour the seasoning on, and then just throw them in the oven. Chicken thighs are easy because you can "overcook" them and they'll still come out juicy and tasty

You can add any seasoning blend you desire. Adobo seasoning is what I grew up with and tastes really good, but I usually use Tony Chachere's Creole Seasoning along with some Chef Paul Prudhomme's Poultry magic, and add garlic and onion powder. This is just a basic template for a meat dish that tastes good and is very easy to do.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray or oil
  • Chicken thighs
  • All-Purpose Seasoning Blend, such as Adobo

Steps

  1. Preheat oven for 425 degrees
  2. Place chicken thighs on aluminum lined baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray
  3. Spray chicken with cooking spray, or coat with oil, and liberally season both sides with the seasoning blend of your choice
  4. Place baking sheet in the oven for 45 minutes, or until chicken is at least 165 degrees internally (Preferably 175, dark meat is delicious "overcooked")
  5. Let rest for 5-10 minutes before eating
